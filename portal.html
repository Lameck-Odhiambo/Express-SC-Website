News & Events

 

The season 14 officially starts on Saturday 13th January 2018 and 14th January for the Sunday training at Hillcrest Pre-school grounds.

All School-Based Training program will officially kick off on 9th January in the respective schools as per their usual schedule.

The Express soccer Development League matches will be held at Hillcrest Pre-school grounds on 17th and 24th March 2018.

Express stars from 7 years old are expected to train on Saturday and Sunday. They will also participate in leagues and friendly matches once every month as indicated in the calendar.

Express soccer will register the Simba and Kifaru
groups in the upcoming Nairobi West leagues
which will be played once a month.

Express soccer academy has introduced mid-week
program on Wednesday. This will cater for players who need extra training.

After participating in a competitive and well-organized tournament in Dubai last year, the academy has decided that it will take another team this year to Dubai, expected to take place towards the end of November. Registration starts in February until end of July.

Express soccer academy did very well in the Arusha Chipukizi December 2017 Tournament. will register teams to participate in Arusha Chipukizi Tournament in December 2018. Registration starts in June and ends in September.
